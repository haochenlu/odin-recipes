Chinese Meatball Soup

A classic Southern style Chinese meatball in wintermelon soup

A classic soup enjoyed all over China, with springy meatballs and a light delectable chicken broth base. This soup is quite ubiquitous in many a Chinese child's memory.

Meatball Ingredients

  • Ground pork: 500g
  • Ginger, minced: 4g
  • Scallion, minced: 1
  • Light Soy Sauce: 2 tbsp
  • Shaoxing Cooking Wine: 2 tbsp
  • Water: 2 tbsp
  • Sesame Oil: 1 tsp
  • Egg White: 1 egg
  • Sugar: 1/2 tsp
  • Ground White Pepper: 1/2 tsp

Soup ingredients

  • Mung Bean Vermicelli: 1 bunch
  • Wintermelon: 500g
  • Cooking Oil: 1 tbsp
  • Scallions: 2
  • Chicken Stock: 1L
  • Water: 500mL
  • Ground White Pepper: To taste
  • Sesame oil: 1/2 tsp
  • Salt: To Taste

Meatball Assembly

  1. Combine all meatball ingredients and whip with chopsticks in one direction until the mixture resists your chopsticks, around 10 minutes
  2. Form meatballs with your hands

Soup Assembly

  1. Soak mung bean vermicelli for at least 10 minutes in cold water
  2. Cut wintermelon into chunks, making sure to remove the thick tough skin
  3. Add oil to stockpot and heat over medium heat, add scallions and fry for 1-2 minutes
  4. Add chicken stock, water and wintermelon allow to come to a boil
  5. Add the meatballs and the rest of the soup ingredients once the soup comes to a boil
